# Mobile Programming Swift – Lab Assignments

SwiftUI lab projects built following the [Hacking with Swift](https://www.hackingwithswift.com) tutorial series.

---

## Lab 6 – WeSplit

A check-splitting app that calculates how much each person owes at a restaurant.

**Features:**
- Enter the total bill amount
- Select number of people splitting the bill
- Choose a tip percentage (10%, 15%, 20%, 25%, or 0%)
- Displays the grand total and amount per person
- Tip of 0% is highlighted in red as a reminder

**Concepts covered:** `Form`, `NavigationStack`, `Picker`, `TextField`, `@State`, `@FocusState`, computed properties, currency formatting

---

## Lab 7 – Guess the Flag

A flag-guessing game that tests your knowledge of world flags.

**Features:**
- Shows 3 random country flags per round
- Tap the flag matching the named country
- Tracks your score across 8 rounds
- Alerts tell you if you were correct or which country the wrong flag belonged to
- Final score shown at game over with option to play again

**Countries included:** Estonia, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Monaco, Nigeria, Poland, Spain, UK, Ukraine, US

**Concepts covered:** `ZStack`, `VStack`, `Button`, `Image`, `Alert`, asset catalogs, `RadialGradient`, custom view structs
